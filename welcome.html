🛡️ Two rules that matter

🚫 Practice never changes your real test scores
Practice runs, the daily challenge, and the leaderboard are for fun and revision. They earn you points and badges. But they NEVER change your chapter test results or your EASE growth line. Real tests = real scores. Practice = practice. We keep these separate on purpose — so you can experiment without stress.
🔒 No shop, no buying, no spending
You can earn points, levels, tiers, and avatar styles. You cannot spend them. There is no shop. There are no things to buy. Everyone gets the same chance and the same look options. We did this on purpose, so the hub stays fair for every student.
One more thing. When you take a test, the screen will block copy-paste and right-click. It will also notice if you switch tabs. That is normal for a real test — not a punishment.

⭐ How points work — the short version

Here is the very simple version. For the full guide, open How Points Work.

  • Points — you earn them by submitting tests, doing practice runs, and finishing daily challenges.
  • Level — your level goes up when you collect enough total points. There is a level table on the How Points Work page.
  • Tier — Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Master. Tier moves up with your level.
  • Streak — how many days in a row you practised. 3 days = +100 bonus. 7 days = +250 bonus. Don't break your streak!

📚 Words you will see

Short, plain meanings for the words that show up most often.

Chapter Test
A short test on one unit (chapter) of your subject. Your teacher schedules it for your class. The score counts.
EASE
A growth test you take 3 times a year — once each term. It adapts to your level: if you get a question right, the next one gets harder. The score lets us see if you grew across the year.
EASE Window
The few weeks when an EASE test is open. You can take it any time inside the window. After the window closes, you cannot take it for that term.
Practice Run
A self-paced session you start yourself. Earns points. Never changes test scores.
Streak
Days in a row you practised. If you skip a day, the streak resets to 0.
Tier
A label for how far you have come overall: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Master. It moves up with your level.
Parent Share Link
A link you can send to your parent so they see your test result. No login needed. You create the link from a result page, and you can delete it any time.
Flagged question
If a question looks wrong to a teacher (broken image, unclear wording), an HQ observer can flag it. You will not see this — it's a behind-the-scenes safety net so test questions stay fair.
Stuck on something?
If something does not work, or feels wrong, tell your teacher. They can flag it for the HQ team. We fix it for everyone.
